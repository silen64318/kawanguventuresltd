Company Overview

Building a Brighter Future with Kawangu Ventures Limited.

Kawangu Ventures Limited, formerly Kawangu Electrical Ventures Limited, established in 1982 and incorporated in 2012, is a distinguished private limited company specializing in Electrical Installations, supply of electrical goods and services, Building and Constructions, and Civil and Road Works.

Our growth trajectory is fueled by a commitment to excellence. We have adopted a policy to admit professionals with proven capabilities at both local and international levels. Our firm boasts an elaborate network of associate consultants, trainers, and business development collaborators. Together, we deliver competitively priced and value-adding services tailored to the specific needs of our clients.

At the heart of our mission is the desire to address the needs of our clients through expertise in development planning, design, construction, and rehabilitation of the living environment of communities throughout Kenya.

Kawangu Ventures is composed of business-minded professionals dedicated to adding value to organizational performance. Our comprehensive range of services spans various sectors of the economy, catering to clients such as the Government of Kenya, parastatals, non-governmental organizations, private sectors, and international organizations.
