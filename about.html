About Kawangu Ventures Limited

Empowering Innovations for Sustainable Growth.

Kawangu Ventures Limited, formerly known as Kawangu Electrical Ventures Limited, is a private limited company that was registered in 1982 and later incorporated in 2012. Specializing in Electrical Installations, supply of electrical goods and services, Building and Constructions, and Civil and Road Works, Kawangu Ventures has been a key player in the construction industry.

Our firm's headquarters are strategically located within the Central Business District in Thika, at the 1st Floor, Everton Building, along Thika/Garissa Road. We are dedicated to delivering top-notch services and value to our clients, which include government bodies, parastatals, non-governmental organizations, private sectors, international organizations, and more.

With a strong emphasis on growth, Kawangu Ventures has experienced unprecedented expansion, thanks to our policy of admitting professionals with proven capabilities on both local and international levels. Our network of associate consultants, trainers, and business development collaborators ensures the delivery of competitively priced and value-adding services tailored to the specific needs of our clients.

At Kawangu Ventures, we believe in operating as a multidisciplinary company, not only in Kenya but also in Africa and select international markets. Our vision is to be the contractor of choice in the construction industry, achieved through a commitment to safety, timeliness, high quality, innovation, and cost-effectiveness.

Our mission is centered around addressing the needs of our clients by utilizing our expertise in development planning, design, construction, and rehabilitation of the living environment of communities throughout Kenya.

Vision & Values

Kawangu Ventures aspires to continuous and sustainable top and bottom-line growth, operating as a desirable place of work that fosters creativity and enthusiasm within a safe environment. Our values include honesty, integrity, open and transparent communication, safety and development of people, professionalism, and quality.

Philosophy

Over the years, Kawangu Ventures has gained respect as one of the most esteemed general contracting firms in Kenya. Our success is attributed to an energetic enthusiasm for our work, sensitivity to customer needs, a willingness to accept total responsibility, and a craftsman-like pride in constructing projects that endure for generations.

Approach to Business

Kawangu provides a full range of services, starting from needs assessment to project design, implementation, management, and maintenance. We aim to contribute to capacity building in the communities where we operate, emphasizing employment creation and knowledge transfer to local communities.

Partnership Approach

Skills transfer and the development of competencies are integral to our work. Kawangu Ventures adopts a partnership approach, involving extensive consultation, communication, and skills transfer to ensure that clients gain ownership of the project and knowledge transfer.

Financial Performance

Kawangu Ventures is a financially sound medium-sized firm, supported by the Directors' financial backing and reputable banking facilities. Our aim is to transition from a medium-sized to a large firm in Kenya and surrounding countries through competence, adherence to high professional standards, and a commitment to total quality management.
